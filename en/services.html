Therapy Services

The Following Therapy Services Are Offered

In-Person

It is when the person has the availability to attend their therapy sessions by meeting with the counselor in the office.

Telehealth

It is the use of a telephone and the use of telecommunication technologies to extend mental health care, when you and your counselor are not in the same place at the same time. If you have a phone or a device such as a computer / laptop with an internet connection and a safe and private place, you already have everything you need to obtain services through telehealth.

Hybrid

It is a combination of both in-person and Telehealth therapy.
