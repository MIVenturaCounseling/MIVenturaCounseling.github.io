Misión

At MIVentura Counseling, our mission is to help you or your loved ones navigate life’s challenges with care, compassion, and evidence-based support. Whether you're seeking help for yourself, your child, your relationship, or your family, we’re here to provide a safe space for growth and healing.

Welcome

MIVentura Counseling is here to provide you with the right supportive environment to assist and help you to freely explore your initial concerns. I can help you discover your internal strengths and resources, and at the same time to support you to stay focused on your goals. By providing you with the skills and tools necessary for you to develop and create your own personal strategies, and thus achieve your goals. Which are based on your own unique needs and these, in turn, can lead you to create a better balance in your life.

If you are experiencing an emotional crisis or you are in danger please call 911.
